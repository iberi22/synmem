### What is SynMem?

SynMem (Synthetic Memory) is a revolutionary browser agent that bridges the gap between AI assistants and the authenticated web. Unlike traditional scraping tools, SynMem lets AI agents use YOUR browser sessions - meaning they can access your logged-in accounts, remember context, and automate complex workflows.

Traditional AI agents are blind to the authenticated web. They can't access your emails, social feeds, or AI conversations. SynMem changes that - securely exposing your browser sessions to AI via the MCP protocol.
