## ðŸ“ Platform-Specific Notes

### Product Hunt Best Practices

1. **Launch Day**: Tuesday or Wednesday for best engagement
2. **Timing**: Launch at 12:01 AM PT (reset time)
3. **Engagement**: Respond to every comment within hours
4. **Authenticity**: Be genuine, not salesy
5. **Updates**: Post updates throughout the day

### Hacker News Tips

1. Don't ask for upvotes (against rules)
2. Be prepared for critical feedback
3. Respond thoughtfully to technical questions
4. Avoid promotional language
5. Focus on technical innovation

### Reddit Guidelines

1. Follow each subreddit's rules exactly
2. Disclose that you're the creator
3. Add value, don't just promote
4. Engage genuinely with comments
5. Don't cross-post the same content
